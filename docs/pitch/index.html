Where we started

A strong business, held back by old technology.

The Psychic Gift has been trading since 2002 with a loyal client base and experienced readers. The website was the bottleneck — actively losing traffic and conversions.

Before — Legacy site

The old platform

  • PHP/WordPress, built circa 2010
  • No mobile optimisation
  • No SEO metadata on any page
  • Reader list out of date (departed readers listed)
  • Broken internal links throughout
  • Wrong phone numbers displayed
  • No structured data for Google
  • No way for Charlotte to update content
  • Site actively crawled but not indexed effectively
After — New platform

What we've built

  • Next.js 15 — fast, modern, scalable
  • Mobile-first, responsive design
  • Per-page SEO metadata, titles, descriptions
  • 20 reader profiles, each individually indexed
  • All internal links working correctly
  • Correct production phone numbers throughout
  • JSON-LD structured data (Google rich results)
  • CMS planned (Sanity + Firestore)
  • Sitemap, robots.txt, canonical URLs — all correct
The platform

Built for growth from day one.

Every technical decision was made with SEO, conversion, and long-term scalability in mind — not just getting something live.

🔍

SEO-ready architecture

Server-rendered pages, correct metadata on every route, structured data for rich results, sitemap including all 20+ reader profiles. Google can index everything.

📱

Mobile-first

Phone numbers visible on mobile across all service pages. Responsive layouts tested across breakpoints. Core Web Vitals optimisation underway.

👤

Reader profiles

Each reader has a dedicated, indexed page with unique title, description, and breadcrumb trail. Real-time availability status shown to visitors.

📞

Conversion-focused

Phone numbers prominent on every page, call-to-action components on all service pages, offer page rebuilt for clarity and CAP compliance.

📊

Analytics & tracking

Google Analytics installed with cookie consent. Conversion tracking (call clicks, modal opens) being added. Full funnel visibility from landing to call initiated.

Growth strategy

Five pillars. One goal: more calls.

Each pillar is independent — they compound over time. SEO builds the foundation, content adds depth, ads bring immediate traffic, CRO maximises every visitor, social builds brand awareness.

01 — SEO

Search & organic traffic

Technical foundations complete. Keyword targets set across head terms, service pages, reader specialties, and long-tail queries. AI agents run weekly to find new opportunities.

Foundation complete
02 — Content

Blog & editorial

Sanity CMS will power a blog targeting informational keywords (love readings, what is clairvoyance, etc.) — high volume, low competition. Content compounds in value over time.

CMS in design
03 — Paid Ads

Google Ads

Landing pages built and ready. Campaign structure planned across brand, high-intent transactional, and specialty keywords. Fastest route to validated traffic while SEO grows.

Ready to launch
04 — CRO

Conversion optimisation

Heatmaps, funnel tracking, and A/B testing to understand what's actually driving calls. Hero CTAs, offer framing, reader card layout — every element testable and measurable.

Tracking setup next
05 — Social

Social media

Reader spotlights, psychic insights, client stories — strong raw material for Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok (#psychictok has billions of views). Drives awareness and word-of-mouth.
